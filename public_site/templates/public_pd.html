What is Action Fractions?

Action Fractions is an integrated mathematics-computational thinking curriculum, designed using Everyday Mathematics. This course focuses on 3rd and 4th grade fractions instruction, providing 10 hours of instruction per year to augment any existing mathematics curriculum.

What's special about Action Fractions?

All materials and PD are free of charge. It provides a variety of integrated lessons that use computing to build fractions fundamentals in 3rd and 4th grade while building computational thinking skills. It provides complete lesson plans, including discussion prompts, reflections, extension ideas, and assessments.
